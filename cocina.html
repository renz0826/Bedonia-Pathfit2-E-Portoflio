Cocina

crevettes au beurre saines

A vibrant mix of sautéed corn kernels, tender mushrooms, and juicy shrimp, garnished with fresh celery. The combination of golden corn, earthy mushrooms, and plump shrimp creates a visually appealing and flavorful meal, perfect for a fresh and satisfying bite.

Ingredients

12 pcs of Fresh Shrimp

1 bowl of Jolly Whole Corn

1 bowl of Jolly Mushrooms

1 stick of Dari Creme Classic Margarine

1 saucer of Celery

1 bulb of Minced Garlic

1/2 table spoon of Salt

1/2 table spoon of Ground Pepper

2 table spoons of Paprika

Preparation

  1. Prepare Your Ingredients
    1. Peel and devein the shrimp. Rinse and pat them dry.
    2. Drain the canned corn thoroughly.
    3. Slice the mushrooms.
    4. Mince one garlic bulb and roughly chop fresh celerey.
    5. Measure out margarine and spices (paprika, salt, and black pepper).
    6. Set everything up (mise en place) before starting.
  2. Sear the Shrimp
    1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat (add a drizzle of canola oil).
    2. Add the shrimp and sear until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes per side.
    3. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  3. Sauté the Aromatics
    1. Lower the heat to medium and add the butter to the pan.
    2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds to 1 minute until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  4. Cook the Vegetables
    1. Add the drained corn and sliced mushrooms to the pan.
    2. Stir to combine and cook for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables soften and begin to caramelize.
    3. Sprinkle in the paprika, salt, and black pepper, stirring to evenly coat.
  5. Combine the Shrimp
    1. Return the seared shrimp to the pan.
    2. Gently toss with the vegetables and cook together for another minute to let the flavors meld.
  6. Finishing Touches
    1. Taste the dish and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
    2. Sprinkle freshly chopped celery on top.
  7. Plate and Serve
    1. For presentation, set some celery leaves on the serving plate.
    2. Transfer the shrimp, corn, and mushroom sauté to the plate.
    3. Serve hot on its own or with rice or crusty bread.
